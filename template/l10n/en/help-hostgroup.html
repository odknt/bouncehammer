Host group
Reserved
RFC2606 or Reserved Top Level DNS Names.
Cellphone
Email domains which are used by mainly cellular phone.
Smartphone
Email domains which are used by mainly smartphone
PC
PC address(domain), ISP, company, organization, and so on
Webmail
Domains used by web-based e-mail service.
e.g. gmail.com, yahoo.com, hotmail.com, aol.com, and such.
Neighbor
Domain names which are defined at the config file: etc/neighbor-domains
Undefined
Undefined host group.
